About

I'm a Senior Product Manager based in Pittsburgh, PA. I've spent 12+ years building products that make a real difference in people's lives.

The thread through my career is simple: I care about building things that matter to real people, and I use data to make sure they actually work. At Human Interest, that meant making sure employees got enrolled in their retirement plans on time and that the data feeding their benefits was accurate. At Bossa Nova Robotics, it meant turning messy robot telemetry into insights that improved how stores keep products on shelves. At VIT Initiative, I built an IoT wearable and software platform from zero to one that reduced back pain and improved wellbeing for employees in warehousing and manufacturing settings.

I'm drawn to problems where the technical complexity is high but the human impact is clear. I've worked across regulated fintech, robotics, IoT, and AI, and in each case the part that motivates me is the same: someone's life gets a little better because the product works. I find the problems through data, design solutions that scale, and ship products that drive measurable business outcomes.

What I'm looking for

I want to work somewhere the product outcomes matter to real people. My best fit is B2B2C fintech, platform infrastructure, or regulated environments where data quality and user impact intersect. I'm targeting Senior PM, Lead PM, and Group PM roles.

I'm remote-preferred or Pittsburgh-local.
